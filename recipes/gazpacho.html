Room temp gazpacho Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • Many tomatoes
  • 2 maybe 5 cucumbers
  • 1/8 bell pepper, any color
  • 4 garlics (so people can smell it on you)
  • 1 glass red wine (for drinking) and 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar (for cooking)
  • Some water
  • Oil, preferably extra-virgin
  • Salt and peppah
  • cubed bread
  • basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine all of it except bread, oil, bread, basil into a blender until it is smooth then add the oil and blend again to combine
  2. Add oil to a large skillet on medium heat to coat the bottom of the pan, add the cubed bread and cock until crisp
  3. Remove from heat and let cool
  4. Let both the gazpacho soup and bread get room temp
  5. Once roomtemp, serve to unsuspecting guests thinking it is ice cold so they will burn their mouth eating it
