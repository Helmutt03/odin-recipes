Sloppy Steaks

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 steak just waiting on getting slopped
  • A lot of salt
  • Some pepper (freshly grounded of course)
  • one tablespoon of oil
  • The biggest glass of water you can find

Steps

  1. Pour up your water glass so you have it ready in time
  2. Add the oil to a skillet and turn on the heat
  3. Lay that big meaty sucker in the skillet when the oil has gotten a bit spicy
  4. Let that sucker sear on both sides until you got a nice crust
  5. Lower the heat and cock until perfectly medium rare
  6. Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before placing it on a platter
  7. Pour the water glass over the steak and get ready for some SLOPPY STEAKS
