Secret Work Hot Dog

Description

Ingredients

  • A hot dog
  • Bread (optional)
  • Condiments (optional)
  • A long sleeved shirt for hiding it during an important meeting

Steps

  1. Go buy a hot dog
  2. Bring said hot dog to work as lunch
  3. Find out you have an important meeting during lunchtime essentially forcing you to skip lunch
  4. Unable to accept this you get the brilliant idea to sneak lunch into the meeting using your longe sleeved shirt
  5. Deny it when your coworkers see you eating
  6. Start choking on the hot dog
  7. Make your coworkers help you not die and then hold a speech on why lunch cannot ever be skipped and that skipping it is a health risk
